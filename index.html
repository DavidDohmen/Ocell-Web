RETHINKING AERIAL ANALYTICS

Ocell is generating, interpreting and delivering actionable data through aerial images. We want to take away the pain of owning and operating drones from customers who just want the insights. Leveraging the latest innovations in AI, sensorics and operations, we unlock profoundly new ways of aerial analytics.

Data Quality

Modern imaging technology and smart processing algorithms ensure the best quality and resolution for all usecases.

Timeliness

Depending on our customer preferences, we can deliver high frequencies of image updates. On the long run we'll even be able to provide real-time feeds.

Cost Savings

Customers don't need to hire certified drone operators or pilots in order to generate aerial imagery anymore. By scaling the process of image capturing, Ocell can provide cheaper data in a more convenient way.

Artificial Intelligence

Ocell uses state of the art in Artificial Intelligence in order to enhance image quality and extract meaningful information from the data. These valuable insights are provided to our customers.
